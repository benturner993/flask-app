Welcome to Retain and Reward

Retain and Reward is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline reactive, proactive, and win back lapse tasks. It serves as a central hub for managing customer interactions and incentive programs, allowing you to effectively retain and reward our customers here at Bupa.

Reactive

Reactive lapse prevention is crucial because it helps in retaining customers who are at risk of leaving due to dissatisfaction or lack of engagement.

Proactive

Proactive lapse prevention helps in identifying potential issues before they occur, allowing you to address them proactively and maintain customer satisfaction.

Win Back

Win back lapse prevention aims to re-engage customers who have previously left your service, providing an opportunity to regain their trust and loyalty.
